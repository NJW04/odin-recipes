Hotdogs:

Description:

Following this recipe will allow you to make delicious hotdogs that look just like this!

Take a sassuage and cook it.

This recipe has been passed down in my family for generations.

Ingredients:

  • Roll
  • Sassuage
  • Little leaf thing

Steps:

  1. Cook the sassuage.
  2. Cut open a slit in the roll.
  3. Insert sassuage into roll.
  4. Enjoy this amazing hotdog!
